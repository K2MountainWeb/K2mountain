Explore K2 Base Camp: For Non-Mountaineers

K2 Base Camp: Journey to heart of the Mountain

The K2 Mountain is not only for mountaineers. It is also an adventerous place and treeking destination for those who want to explore the beauty of K2 and Karakoram Range without attempting summit of K2 Mountian.The treak to K2 mountain offers non-mountaineers to be in the heart of second highest mountain in the world.

The treeking Experience: Trek Difficulty

The trek to base camp of K2 mountain is not a climb. But
The difficulty includes:

  • Rocky paths
  • Cross glaciers
  • High-altitude conditions

But trek is not recommended for those with medical conditions.

Altitude of Base Camp

The hight of base camp is 5150 meters (16896 feet), which is high but not difficult. The Duration of trek The trek lasts around 10 to 14 days.

Best Time: For Non-Mountaneers

The best time for trek of K2 base camp is during summer months (June to September). At this time the weather is more stable. But trek is not recommended in Winter because of harsh weather and snow fall.
